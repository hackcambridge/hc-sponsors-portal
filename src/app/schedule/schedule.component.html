Schedule

Run-of-Show for Hack Cambridge

Friday 19th January

10:00-18:00

We will be setting up the venue. Sponsors are welcome to deliver any equipment or swag on this day that they wish to but it must arrive between 10:00-18:00, as they are the only times we have the venue. We are unable to take responsiblity for anything you leave overnight in the venue.

People can also come along to set up or look around the venue, but please let us know in advance.

Saturday 20th January

    08:00

  • Doors open for sponsors to set up
  • You are welcome to arrive later if you wish
  • Previous schedule said 08:30, but it has been pushed back
  • All trestle tables will already be set up for you before you arrive

    09:30

  • Doors open for hackers
  • We expect most hackers to arrive closer to 10am, but some will arrive early
  • Hackers tend to hang around the sponsors' area looking for swag between this time and the opening ceremony. It is a good chance to speak to them at your stalls.

    11:00

  • Opening Ceremony happens in Corn Exchange main hall
  • Sponsors not participating are welcome to watch
  • Run of show:
    • 11:00—Welcome from Hack Cambridge Team
    • 11:05—Cambridge Consultants presentation
    • 11:10—Improbable presentation
    • 11:15—MLH presentation
    • 11:25—Mentoring System Explained
    • 11:30—Microsoft presentation
    • 11:35—Sophos presentation
    • 11:40—Winding down presentation
    • 11:45—Presentation ends

    12:00

  • Hacking starts
  • It is a good idea during hacking for mentors to proactively speak to hackers and offer help
  • There will also be a ticketing system to match hackers and mentors
  • Lunch is served in Food Area until 13:00
  • This is right next to the main sponsor stalls, so you should get a lot of attention!

    13:00

  • Product Demos start
  • These happen in the Workshop Room in the GuildHall, and are short tech-focussed talks
  • We will announce these on stage just before each one begins
  • Schedule is not fixed, as not all sponsors have confirmed their slots:
    • 13:00—Amazon
    • 13:15—Microsoft
    • 11:30—Unconfirmed
    • 11:45—Unconfirmed
    • 14:00—Unconfirmed
    • 14:15—Unconfirmed
    • 14:30—Product Demos finish

    14:40

  • Workshops start
  • These happen in the Workshop Room in the GuildHall, and are short tech-focussed talks
  • Will announce these on stage just before each one starts
  • Schedule is not fixed, as not all sponsors have confirmed their slots:
    • 14:40—Google
    • 15:15—Sophos (Unconfirmed)
    • 15:50—Improbable
    • 16:25—Cambridge Consultants
    • 17:00—Microsoft
    • 17:35—ARK.io
    • 18:03—Workshops finish

    18:45

  • DataScope gives pre-dinner speech
  • 15 minutes on the Main Stage

    19:00

  • Dinner is served in the Food Area by sponsor stalls
  • Again, expect lots of footfall!

    20:00

  • Sponsors' reception
  • This is our "thank you" to our sponsors, where we will provide drinks and snacks
  • In St John's bar, which is in the Corn Exchange
  • Hackers will be participating in the MLH Capture the Flag competition

    21:00

  • TPP give a brief presentation on the Main Stage

    21:15

  • MLH run their !Light game

    22:00

  • Sleeping area opens for Hackers
  • This is next door to the sponsors' stalls and Food Area
  • Many hackers will choose to work through the night instead

    23:45

  • FeatureSpace give their Midnight Snack speech
  • 15 minutes on the Main Stage

Sunday 21st January

    00:00

  • Midnight Snack. Pizza will be given to hackers in the Food Area.
  • Mentors are not expected to stay all night. You can stay if you wish, or leave well before this time. We recommend mentors be back for 09:00 since people are likely to want assistance in the final few hours leading to their project completion.

    08:30

  • Breakfast is served to hackers

    11:00

  • Accenture give a short presentation
  • Main Stage

    12:00

  • Hacking stops
  • Judging briefing starts for sponsors in St John's bar, within the Corn Exchange. Here, we will explain how judging works to sponsors who want to participate.
  • Lunch is served to hackers
  • Hacking room is set up for expo

    12:45

  • Expo starts
  • Sponsors and Hack Cambridge team begin judging

    14:15

  • Expo finishes
  • Finalists are announced

    14:30

  • Finalists give their presentations

    16:00

  • Headline judges choose winners and runners up

    16:15

  • Closing ceremony begins. Timings are still being decided.
    • 16:15—Event summary

    • 16:30—Unconfirmed. Waiting on sposnors to provide further information about prizes.
    • 16:45—Hack Cambridge winners announced
    • 17:00—End

  • Venue is completely closed to hackers at 18:00

    17:30

  • Clean-up begins

    19:00
