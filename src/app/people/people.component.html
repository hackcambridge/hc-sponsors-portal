People

Tell us who you will be coming to Hack Cambridge.

Everyone coming to Hack Cambridge will be added to our Slack channel before the event so you can chat to the hackers. You will also be added to HELPq, a ticketing system built by HackMIT specifically for hackathons. To do so we need to know who is coming in advance. Phone numbers will only be used if we need to contact you during the event urgently.

Mentors

You may bring an unlimited number of mentors.

Mentors should have technical backgrounds, as they role is to help hackers with problems they are having with their projects.

Hack Cambridge Judge

Nominate one mentor to be a Hack Cambridge judge.

Hack Cambridge judges take part in deciding who gets the overall Hack Cambridge prizes. If you would like to take part in this, nominate one of your mentors. Note that this does not mean anything for prizes which are run by you – you may run your own competitions as you wish. Judging will be done using Gavel, an expo judging system originally developed for HackMIT.
