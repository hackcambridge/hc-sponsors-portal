People

Everyone coming to Hack Cambridge will be added to our Slack channel so you can chat to the hackers. You will also be added to HELPq, a hackathon ticketing system. To do so we need to know who is coming in advance. Phone numbers will only be used if we need to contact you during the event urgently.

Everyone can take part in deciding who gets the overall Hack Cambridge prizes. Judging will be done using Gavel, an expo judging system originally developed for HackMIT. Note that any competitions your company runs are separate and may be judged however you wish.
