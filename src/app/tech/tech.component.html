Let hackers know what you have to offer

We will provide a list of the tech your company produces to all hackers before the event, so that they can use it too.

You may list as many pieces of tech that your company produces, and we will advertise it before the hackathon. This is a great opportunity to increase adoption of your products ahead of the hackathon, but please bear in mind items you list should be related to soft/hardware engineering and design, not generic commercial products. If you have lots of relevant items, this may be more overwhelming than helpful; we recommend you focus on the perhaps 3 or 4 technologies that you think may be most useful for a weekend hackathon project. If you are running any competitions, workshops or side-events, be sure to list any related tech!
